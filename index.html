  • ONLY HUMANOpen or Close

    Film Festival Poster Series + Intro Video

    Only Human Film Festival Posters are the first part of the series. It comes in 4 posters but also a video that is currently being created. The festival follows of theme that we all make mistakes, and we should embrace them. The posters cover 3 films: Outcasts, Perfection, and Elephant Man.

    Second part of the festival is a video intro that was made to entail what the festival was about. It follows the theme of "mistakes are mean't to be forgiven".

  • CODE NOWOpen or Close

    Rebranding project on an existing nonprofit organization called CodeNow. Ths nonprofit promotes interests and possible career into computer science for young students who are underprivileged. They offer afterschool programs to teach kids about programming.

    The rebranding exercise is done to further interest kids to join the program by providing them with cool and fun notebooks and ID badges to attend a programming workshop.

    The website is also revamped for ease of access for students and parents. The logo is changed to make coding more fun and cool.
